Early Access · Patch 0.5.0

Return of the Ancients

A complete rework of Path of Exile 2's endgame, featuring the new Runes of Aldur league, Atlas overhaul, and game-changing mechanics updates.

Release: May 21, 2026
Duration: 3 months

🔮 Runes of Aldur — New League

The Runes of Aldur league introduces a new endgame mechanic centered on ancient rune fragments scattered across maps. Collect and combine runes to unlock powerful rewards, boss encounters, and rare item drops.

Runes have three tiers of rarity: common (gray), uncommon (blue), and rare (gold). Combining matching sets grants cumulative bonuses and unlocks special boss encounters tied to ancient lore.

Rune Collection Core Mechanic

Find rune fragments in any map. Each rune has unique properties and synergies with specific ascendancies.

Altar Crafting

Use runes at special altars to craft rare items, guarantee specific mods, or reroll item properties with rune power.

Ancient Bosses

Unlock fights against legendary ancients by completing rune sets. Defeat them for guaranteed unique drops and exotic rewards.

🗺️ Atlas & Endgame Overhaul

The Atlas has been completely restructured. Passive skill trees are now map-specific, allowing you to customize rewards per tier or region. The Vaal Temple endgame boss fight now scales dynamically based on your build's damage and survivability.

New "Pinnacle" encounters appear randomly in tier 14+ maps. These optional boss fights reward exclusive cosmetics, currency, and guaranteed rare uniques.

Regional Passives New

Each Atlas region now has its own passive tree that modifies map rewards, rare spawns, and boss mechanics.

Vaal Temple Rework

Endgame boss now scales to your gear. Higher survivability = harder fight, better rewards.

Pinnacle Encounters

Random endgame bosses with unique cosmetic rewards. Purely optional but highly rewarding.

Ascendancy Nexus

New endgame boss themed around combining multiple ascendancies. Requires strategic planning and diverse builds.

⚙️ League Mechanic Reworks

Several legacy league mechanics have been rebalanced for patch 0.5.0:

Delve 2.0

Azurite nodes now grant build-specific passive points. Deeper delves unlock hidden rare mods.

Breach Evolved

Breaches now spawn unique elite packs. Closing a breach grants a temporary buff stacking up to 5x.

Blight Expansion

New tower types for end-game Blight encounters. Combines active gameplay with tower defense.

Harvest Consolidation

Harvest crafts now guarantee specific mod outcomes. Less RNG, more player agency.

⚡ New Ascendancies

Two new ascendancies have been added to the game. The Chrono-Weaver (Witch) focuses on time manipulation and haste mechanics. The Stone Warden (Marauder) specializes in earth and defensive synergies.

Chrono-Weaver Witch

Master temporal abilities. Slow enemies, accelerate allies, and create time-locked zones with scaling damage.

Stone Warden Marauder

Summon animated terrain and earth golems. Gain armor scaling from golem count and active minions.

💎 Unique Items & Quality of Life

18 new unique items have been introduced, with a focus on supporting the new ascendancies and rune mechanic. Additionally, several quality-of-life improvements ship with 0.5.0:

  • Improved trade interface with saved searches
  • Vendor recipes now display required items in tooltips
  • New minimap filters for league content
  • Performance optimizations for large spell effect packs

📅 Timeline

Patch 0.5.0 launches on May 21, 2026. All current league content will be migrated to the core game. Existing characters remain intact.
